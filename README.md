# 📚 Research Paper Summarizer & Gap Finder

> AI-Powered Literature Review and Research Gap Discovery Platform

## 📌 Overview

Research Paper Summarizer & Gap Finder is an AI-powered platform that helps students, researchers, and academicians analyze research papers efficiently. Users can upload multiple papers, and the system automatically summarizes findings, compares methodologies, extracts key insights, and identifies potential research gaps.

The platform significantly reduces the time required for literature reviews and assists researchers in discovering novel thesis and project ideas.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Researchers spend countless hours:

* Reading lengthy papers
* Comparing methodologies
* Identifying research gaps
* Tracking citations
* Understanding trends

This manual process is time-consuming and prone to oversight.

The platform automates literature review and accelerates research discovery using AI.
